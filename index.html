Add to Home Screen

iPhone / iPad (Safari)
Open the app in Safari. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow). Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen. Give it a name and tap Add.

Android (Chrome)
Open the app in Chrome. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner. Tap Add to Home screen. Confirm the name and tap Add.

The app will appear on your home screen like any other app and open full-screen, without the browser bar.

Add to PC

Chrome or Edge
Open the app in your browser. Look for the install icon in the address bar — a small screen with a download arrow. Click it and select Install. The app will open in its own window and appear in your Start menu or taskbar.

Pinning to taskbar (any browser)
Open the app, right-click the tab, and choose Pin tab to keep it always accessible. Or drag the address bar URL to your desktop to create a shortcut.

No download required — these are web apps that run entirely in your browser.

Protect Your Data

These are web-based apps. Your saved data — conversations, settings, countdowns, and preferences — is stored locally in your browser using cookies and local storage.

Clearing your browser history, cookies, or site data will permanently erase everything. There is no account, no cloud backup, and no way to recover it.

To keep your data safe: do not use private or incognito mode, avoid clearing cookies for these sites, and if you use multiple browsers or devices, be aware that data does not carry over between them.

If you want to preserve something important — a conversation, a reflection, a note — copy it somewhere you own before closing the app.
