📱 Home Screen
Add to Home Screen
iPhone / iPad: Open the app in Safari. Tap the Share button, scroll down, and tap Add to Home Screen. Tap Add to confirm.

Android: Open the app in Chrome. Tap the three-dot menu and tap Add to Home screen. The app will appear on your home screen and open full-screen, without the browser bar.
🖥️ Add to PC
Add to PC
Chrome or Edge: Open the app in your browser. Look for the install icon in the address bar — a small screen with a download arrow. Click it and select Install. The app opens in its own window and appears in your Start menu.

Any browser: Right-click the tab and choose Pin tab, or drag the address bar URL to your desktop to create a shortcut.

🔒 Your Data
Protect Your Data
If you are testing an app in its web-based form, please remember that your saved data — conversations, settings, countdowns, and preferences — are stored locally in your browser.

Clearing your cookies or browser history will permanently erase everything. There is no account, no cloud backup, and no way to recover it. Avoid private or incognito mode, and do not clear site data for these apps.
